About

Platform leadership for global asset management.

Senior Product Manager and CFA Charterholder with extensive experience building enterprise investment platforms within global asset management.

I lead multiple squads delivering core platform services, analytics, and AI-enabled tooling supporting thousands of users across Spectrum. My focus is on shared capabilities that make portfolio workflows consistent across investment, client service, and operations — reducing fragmentation and raising the quality bar across 100+ applications.

My work spans roadmap ownership, prioritisation, OKRs, and platform governance. I influence product teams across the estate to adopt strategic shared products, tooling, and processes, ensuring users have a consistently high-quality experience wherever they work in Spectrum.

I operate as part of a Product, Engineering, and Design triad to deliver customer-focused solutions at enterprise scale with high resiliency. My background across Multi-Asset Solutions and Infrastructure — combined with an MSc in Computer Science (Distinction) from the University of Bath — informs how I think about operating models, migration complexity, and the technical detail that separates good platform delivery from average.
