About Fenimore Harper

Built from public-interest communications, digital research, and real-world counter-misinformation work.

Fenimore Harper was founded in 2021 by Marcus Beard after roles across HM Treasury, the Cabinet Office, and 10 Downing Street. During the COVID-19 period he led the UK Government’s digital counter-misinformation work and advised international partners including NATO and G7 governments. Before government, he launched viral and social content work inside Brandwatch.

That mix of government response, digital media instincts, and public-facing research is what makes the brand distinctive. This site rebuild leans into that instead of hiding it behind generic consultancy language.
