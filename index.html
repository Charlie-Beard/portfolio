How it works

The stack is built for speed, context, and responsible escalation.

Public information about Fenimore Harper consistently points to a workflow that mixes broad ingestion, AI-assisted triage, and human-led judgement. This version packages that into a clear operating model clients can buy and trust.

01

Monitor and cluster

Signals are collected from priority channels, grouped into narratives, and scored for relevance, virality, and harm.

02

Flag synthetic risk

Suspicious assets, coordination patterns, and misinformation markers are separated from ordinary audience chatter.

03

Deliver action

Stakeholders receive concise briefings with the story, why it matters, and what to do next while the window to act is still open.
