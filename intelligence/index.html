FAQ

Questions teams ask before they switch on intelligence.

Is this a replacement for existing monitoring tools?

No. The product is designed to sit on top of your current stack, distilling what matters and surfacing the narratives, risks, and recommended actions your team actually needs.

Can the output match our internal briefing style?

Yes. The product story publicly emphasises customisation, so this build includes tone, structure, source, and escalation choices as part of onboarding.

How does Fenimore Harper handle misinformation or deepfakes?

By combining broad monitoring, synthetic-media awareness, and human review. The goal is not just detection but deciding what kind of organisational response is warranted.
