About

Product leadership for enterprise platforms in asset management.

I am a Senior Product Manager with extensive experience building enterprise investment platforms within global asset management.

I currently lead multiple squads delivering core platform services, analytics, and AI-enabled tooling supporting thousands of users across Spectrum. My focus is on building shared capabilities that make portfolio workflows more consistent across investment, client service, and operations teams.

My work spans roadmap ownership, prioritisation, OKRs, and platform governance. I influence product teams across the platform to use strategic core products, tooling, and processes so users have a consistently high-quality experience in Spectrum.

I work in close partnership with Engineering and Design leads, operating as a triad to deliver customer-focused solutions to real problems at enterprise scale with high resiliency. My earlier experience across Multi-Asset Solutions and Infrastructure still shapes how I think about platform migration, operating models, and delivery detail.
