## Problem
With Spectrum supporting thousands of internal users and 100+ applications, platform teams needed better visibility into product usage and user experience across the estate.

Without shared telemetry and analytics, it was harder to understand how core platform products were performing and where experience improvements would have the greatest impact.

## Approach
Telemetry and Analytics was positioned as one of the core platform capabilities in my roadmap. I worked on it as a strategic shared product rather than as a feature set for a single team.

The focus was on giving product teams better tools and processes to understand experience quality across Spectrum, while ensuring the capability fit the broader platform governance model.

## Solution
The product included telemetry and analytics capabilities with Pendo.io integration, giving Spectrum a more structured approach to platform insight. Rather than relying on fragmented local reporting, teams had access to a more strategic shared capability for understanding usage and experience signals.

### Product decisions
- Built analytics as a core platform service so it could support many products across Spectrum
- Anchored the solution around shared tooling and processes rather than one-off team instrumentation
- Connected telemetry work to user experience quality and governance outcomes

## Impact
- Added telemetry and analytics capability to the set of strategic products underpinning Spectrum
- Improved visibility across a large, multi-application platform environment
- Supported better governance and more informed product decisions across teams

This work helped turn platform insight into a reusable product capability rather than a collection of disconnected reporting efforts.
